About - Cornwall Caravan Holidays

Cornwall Caravan Holidays - "Ruby" is a privately owned caravan sited at Lanyon Holiday Park, Loscombe Lane, Four Lanes, Redruth, Cornwall, TR16 6LP.
Ruby's owners are Cornish born and bred, however live 'Up Country' (meaning, anywhere further north than Cornwall).

Ruby and Lanyon Park is our home when we come to visit in the Spring and late Summer. Waking up to the warm Cornish sunrise, on a quiet family campsite, listening to the early morning risers and wondering what adventures Cornwall has in store for the day .. is our idea of paradise.

It is important to advise any potential customers that Ruby Caravan is not owned or operated by Lanyon Holiday Park.

Ruby was purchased, and was made available for holiday rental, in the summer of 2021.
Situated 2 miles from Cornwall's A30 (Primary Trunk Road), Ruby Caravan offers a great base for people wanting to explore the beautiful county of Cornwall.
It is important to advise any potential customers that it is not owned by Lanyon Holiday Park.

Available for rental, all year round (well, almost) .. Ruby is the perfect luxury holiday home for Trek Walkers, Country Cyclists, Romantic Couples and Family Sun Worshippers
On site is a fabulus play area for the children, as well as an indoor swimming area, games area and campsite shop.
Lanyon Holiday Park is truly one of those hidden beauties that people love to visit, bringing their families and friends.

It's no surprise that the Cornish coastline is one of the main attractions to Cornwall.
Portreath is only 10mins drive away.
Porthtowan and Gwithian's 3 Miles of Golden Sands are both only 20mins.
Whilst a trip to St. Ives should only take about 30mins .. a trip well worth making.
