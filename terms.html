Booking Terms

Cornwall Caravan Holidays - Booking Terms

  1. All accommodation are offered subject to availability upon receipt of a booking request and deposit.
    • A 25% deposit is payable at the time of booking.
    • Deposit becomes non-refundable after 48 hours.
    • Balance due 4 weeks before arrival.
    • £100 damage deposit is also added to caravan. Refundable within 2 weeks of departure, subject to no damage.
    • We will deem the holiday to be cancelled, if balance payment is not made 4 weeks prior to arrival.
  2. Should the accommodation be unavailable due to circumstances that are reasonably within our control, the deposit, and if applicable the full amount will be refunded.
  3. Cancellations must be advised in writing. Cancellations made between 2 and 4 weeks of arrival will be refunded 50% of the total, less the deposit.
  4. The number of persons occupying the accommodation must not exceed the number of berths stated. Persons occupying the accommodation include persons sleeping or regularly eating in the accommodation.
  5. We cannot accept bookings for unaccompanied persons under the age of 25 years, or from all male/ female groups, except by prior arrangement.
  6. Caravan is available from 3.00pm to 8.00pm. There are no arrivals after 8.00pm unless agreed in advance. Please contact us if you are arriving later or your booking may be deemed cancelled.
  7. Caravan must be vacated by 10.00am on the day of departure.
  8. Caravan must be left in a clean and tidy condition. We must be notified of all breakages and damages. At the time of final payment a cleaning/damage deposit of £100 is applied for caravan reservations.
  9. The proprietors or their representatives reserve the right to enter Caravan at any reasonable time.
  10. Bed linen is available on request. If not requested guests must provide their own linen.
  11. Bed linen charged at £12 a double set and £6 a single set.
  12. No towels are provided nor offered. Please ensure you bring your own towels.
  13. We do not accept pets.
  14. Cornwall Caravan Holidays does not accept responsibility for any injury, loss or damage, to any person, personal property or motor vehicles, however arising.
  15. Cornwall Caravan Holidays reserves the right to refuse any booking application or to refuse or terminate accommodation at their absolute discretion.
  16. Cornwall Caravan Holidays shall not be liable in the event that accommodation or advertised facilities or amenities shall not be available due to circumstances beyond their control, this includes storm damage, civil unrest, acts of war and pandemic.
  17. Please check your accommodation thoroughly before departure. Any items unclaimed after one week can be disposed of.
  18. No person under the age of 17 must be left unaccompanied/ unsupervised on the park.
  19. By making a booking with Cornwall Caravan Holidays you are deemed to have accepted our terms and conditions.
  20. We recommend holiday insurance.

If you are unhappy with the service you received from us, please contact us and we will try to resolve your concern.
